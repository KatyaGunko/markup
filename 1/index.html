Mobile security for a connected world

Lookout protects private citizens and the world's largest enterprises with intelligence from 100 million mobile sensors.

Mobile security matters.
Your mobile devices have access to some of your most sensitive personal and professional information. The amount of sensitive information accessible by mobile devices has increased significantly in the past two years and there are no signs of it slowing down. This data makes your devices extremely valuable, and a target for attackers.
Protect all your mobile devices with lookout
The time is now.
Mobile devices are the new frontier for cyber attacks. Highly sophisticated attacks like the Pegasus spyware and man-in-the-middle attacks like the one demonstrated by Lookout on 60 Minutes prove the case that mobile threats can lead to catastrophic data loss. Protecting mobile data on devices and beyond, is more urgent than ever.
Protect your organization now
Lookout is a unique security company.
Lookout has taken a mobile-first approach to security since 2007, and is now trusted with protecting more than 100 million devices – the basis of our unique mobile sensor network – that offers unmatched visibility into the mobile threat landscape for millions of people and some of the world's most security focused companies.
